Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kinetic Reps collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and password (hashed) for authentication
  • Workout Data: Exercise progress, rank completion, reps, and sets
  • Diet Objectives: Hydration, fruit, and vegetable check completion status
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version for app optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To track your fitness progress and rank advancement
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data
  • To provide personalized workout recommendations
  • To send notifications about daily objectives (if enabled)

3. Photos and Camera

Kinetic Reps uses your camera for diet objective verification (e.g., water bottle detection). Photos are:

  • Processed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit
  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • Not stored after processing

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and analytics
  • Google ML Kit — On-device image labeling for diet objectives

5. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Passwords are never stored in plaintext

6. Your Rights

  • Delete Account: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time in Settings
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your workout history via email
  • Opt Out: Disable notifications in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Kinetic Reps is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
