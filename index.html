Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FitDiv collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:
Email address and authentication information used to create and secure your account.

Workout Data:
Exercise progress, completed workouts, ranks, repetitions, sets, achievements, and other fitness-related activity.

Diet Objectives:
Hydration, fruit, vegetable, and other nutrition objective completion data.

Device Information:
Device type, operating system version, and technical information used to improve app performance and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Track your fitness progress and rank advancement.
  • Authenticate your account and protect your data.
  • Provide personalized workout experiences and recommendations.
  • Track objectives, achievements, and app progression.
  • Send notifications related to workouts and objectives if enabled.

3. Photos and Camera Usage

FitDiv uses your camera for diet and objective verification features, such as hydration and food detection.

  • Images may be processed using on-device machine learning technology.
  • Photos are not uploaded to our servers for processing.
  • Photos are not permanently stored after verification.

4. Third-Party Services

FitDiv uses:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database services, cloud infrastructure, and analytics.
  • Google ML Kit — On-device machine learning for image-based diet verification.

5. Data Security

  • Data encryption during transmission using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure authentication methods.
  • Firebase security rules to restrict unauthorized access.
  • Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can delete your account and associated data through app settings.
  • You can request a copy of your account data.
  • You can disable notifications through device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FitDiv is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
